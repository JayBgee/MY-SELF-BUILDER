Workout Routine
Pull-up bar + dumbbells + bodyweight. 30–40 mins before dinner.
Push Day 🔥
Chest · Shoulders · Triceps — ~35 mins
PUSH
💡 Wide = chest, diamond = triceps. Slow negatives on the way down — that's where gains happen.
Pull Day 💪
Back · Biceps — ~35 mins
PULL
💡 Can't do pull-ups? Jump up and lower slowly. Even 3 reps per set builds serious strength fast.
Legs + Core 🦵
Quads · Glutes · Abs — ~30 mins
LEGS
💡 Never skip legs. More testosterone = more growth everywhere. Add dumbbells when bodyweight gets easy.
😴
Active Rest Day
No lifting. 20-min walk after dinner. Muscles grow during rest, not training. Hydrate and sleep early.
Full Body 💥
Compounds — ~40 mins
FULL
💡 Hardest session. You rested Thursday — push through. Compound moves burn most and build most.
Core + Cardio 🏃
Abs · Stamina — ~30 mins
CORE
💡 Strong core = less back pain on long class days. Walk 15 mins after if you have energy.
🙏
Full Rest Day
Church, family, GF — recharge your soul. The grind needs a rested version of you on Monday.
